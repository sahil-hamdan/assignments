Uunchai

created by Sahil Hamdan

Uunchai is an upcoming Indian Hindi-language drama film written and directed by Sooraj Barjatya and produced by Rajshri Productions, in association with Boundless Media and Mahaveer Jain Films. The film stars an ensemble cast of Amitabh Bachchan, Parineeti Chopra, Anupam Kher, Boman Irani, Danny Denzongpa, Neena Gupta and Sarika in prominent roles. It is scheduled for theatrical release on 11 November 2022

Production

It was announced in October 2021 that Sooraj Barjatya will return to the director's seat after six years with Uunchai. Uunchai, like most Rajshri films, is expected to be a family drama, which revolves around the friendship of senior citizens.[6] The film is jointly produced by Rajshri Productions, Boundless Media and Mahaveer Jain Films.[7] It was announced in September 2021 that Amitabh Bachchan, Anupam Kher, Boman Irani and Danny Denzongpa will play the lead roles of four friends along with Neena Gupta in an important role.[8][9] It was announced in October 2021, that Parineeti Chopra will play the lead role of a tourist guide in the film.[10] Later Sarika[11] and Nafisa Ali joined the cast in prominent roles.[12]

Release

Uunchai is the 60th film under Rajshri Productions.[20] It is scheduled for theatrical release on 11 November 2022
